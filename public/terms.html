Network Manager Terms of Use

Network Manager is provided for authorized safeguarding, family-network, and organizational practice use. Access must be approved by the organization administrator.

Use of the app

  • Use the app only for legitimate organizational safeguarding work.
  • Keep sign-in access secure and do not share accounts.
  • Follow your organization’s policy, legal basis, and record-keeping rules.
  • Avoid entering unnecessary identifying or excessive personal information.

Account control

Organization administrators may pause, restore, or remove account access where operationally necessary. Access may also be withdrawn if the account is inactive, unauthorized, or outside the intended use of the app.

Commercial terms

Pricing and activation for this app are handled directly with the administrator rather than through public self-serve checkout.
