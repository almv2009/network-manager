Network Manager Privacy Notice

Network Manager is intended for organization-authorized safeguarding work. It stores operational case, network, invitation, support, and billing information so organizations can manage access, safeguard records, and maintain oversight.

What information may be stored

  • Organization and user account details
  • Case and network planning records
  • Documents, journal entries, invitations, and audit events
  • Support requests and billing-related contact information

How it is used

  • To provide access to the app and control permissions
  • To support safeguarding planning, review, and accountability
  • To keep an audit trail of important administrative actions
  • To process support and commercial requests

Operational responsibility

Your organization is responsible for deciding what information is appropriate to enter, how long it should be retained, and what lawful basis applies. Users should avoid entering unnecessary identifying information.
